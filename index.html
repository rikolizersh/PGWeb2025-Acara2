About East Lombok
East Lombok (Lombok Timur)

East Lombok Regency (Kabupaten Lombok Timur) is a regency of the Indonesian province of West Nusa Tenggara. Located on the eastern part of Lombok island, its capital is the town of Selong. The regency is a land of stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage, home to the majestic Mount Rinjani, the second-highest volcano in Indonesia. It also boasts breathtaking coastlines, including the famous Pink Beach (Pantai Tangsi), and provides a deep look into the traditional life of the Sasak people.
