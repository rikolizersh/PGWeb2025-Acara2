New Eridu

Welcome to the official profile of New Eridu, the First City of the New Dawn and the administrative capital of the Federal Directorate. New Eridu stands as the ultimate expression of human resilience, ingenuity, and collective purpose. Forged from the lessons of a receding past, our city is a beacon of order, prosperity, and sustainable progress for all its inhabitants. This Sites provides a foundational overview of our city's structure and guiding principles.
